# The 7-Step Deployment Checklist (used by Figure, Tesla, Unitree)

1. Safety case document (ISO/TS 15066 compliant)
2. Third-party risk assessment
3. Emergency response plan
4. Operator training certification
5. Data privacy policy + consent forms
6. Bias audit report
7. Incident reporting hotline

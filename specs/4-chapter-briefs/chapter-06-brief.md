# Chapter 06 Brief: Bipedal Locomotion & Whole-Body Control

## Key Content Points

- **Theoretical Foundations**: Complete coverage of ZMP, Divergent Component of Motion, and Capture Point theory with mathematical implementation

- **MPC Walking Controller**: Open-source 2025 baseline convex MPC walking controller that actually works, with tuning parameters and validation

- **RL Alternatives**: Implementation of DreamerV3, PPO, and Diffusion Policy for locomotion with comparison of performance characteristics

- **State-of-the-Art Comparison**: Analysis of 2024-2025 open controllers (RAISE, Orbax, LeggedGym) with practical implementation guidance

- **Whole-Body Dynamics**: Inverse dynamics with task-space priorities using kinematic chains and force control

- **Terrain Adaptation**: Techniques for robust walking over rough terrain with adaptation algorithms and stability measures

# Chapter 03 Brief: Modeling Humanoids: URDF, SRDF & MoveIt 2

## Key Content Points

- **Complete Humanoid Model**: Full URDF + SRDF for a modern 2025 humanoid with 36 DoF example, including proper inertial parameters and open-source licensing

- **Modeling Fundamentals**: Detailed explanation of floating-base vs fixed-base conventions, collision vs visual meshes, and joint limit specifications

- **MoveIt 2 Setup**: Complete configuration from scratch with OMPL, Pilz, and CHOMP planners, including configuration files and parameter tuning

- **Real-Time Planning Scene**: Implementation of planning scene monitors and collision object updates for dynamic environments with ROS 2 integration

- **Interactive RViz2 Tools**: Setup of interactive markers for end-effector control in RViz2 with visualization and control feedback

- **Validation Workflows**: Procedures to validate URDF/SDRF models and MoveIt configurations for humanoid robots
